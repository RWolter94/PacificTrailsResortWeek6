Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or joinone of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ochean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for birb watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week
